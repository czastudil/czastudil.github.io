Project Description

CampusPartner is an ongoing project focused on assistive technology and pedestrian navigation around James Madison University's campus. I realized there was a need for an application like this because the campus is very hilly (there are pros and cons to being nestled in the mountains by the Shenandoah Valley), and visitors or people who are recently mobility impaired may not know accessible routes around campus. This is where my work comes in. I am using Open Street Maps and Open Route Service to pull map annotations and routes that abide by a user's mobility requirements and creates pedestrian routes for them to follow. This is very much a work in progress, but you can see some of the work I have presented at the links below.
